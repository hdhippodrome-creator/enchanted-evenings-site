About the Experience

Enchanted Evenings is an intimate, multi‑room journey inspired by the gothic spirit of the Crescent City. Guests are guided between short performances featuring seasoned storytellers and signature cocktails.

  • Approx. 90 minutes
  • Small group immersion
  • Themed cocktail menu
  • French Quarter venues
